kiddiewink orphanage


KIDDIEWINK orphanage was founded in 2003 with only 5 children as its part. Mrs.Preeti Singh wanted to work for the welfare and betterment of children so she initiatively started this orphanage.She wanted to provide the homeless orphans with a home and a better life.

This small initiative which was started in a one room space is now home to more than 100 orphans with multiple facilities and more space. The KW trust now handles all the affairs of the orphanage and it always urges at improving facilities for our children. The orphanage works mainly in Maharashtra and has its franchise in Assam,Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh. The special thing about are orphanage is that it is completely handled by Women whom all our children call mothers.


our mission

The mission of our orphanage is to make every child of our orphanage independent and educated. It aims at providing its children with life skills and good values and make them better civilians. We want our children to to be successful. The orphanage aims at fulfilling the hunger of every child not just for food, but for love, for home and for safety as well. It wishes that every homeless child gets a home. It is not always easy, it comes with a long list of challenges...... but our hardworking staff and volunteers are always trying to overcome all those hurdles.
